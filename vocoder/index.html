airloom

how to play

sing, hum, or talk into your mic — airloom reshapes your voice into the instrument. best with headphones, in a quiet room, in chrome.

right hand

  • move up / down → pitch (snaps to your key)
  • pinch thumb + index → hear your natural voice
  • out of frame → silence

left hand

  • fist ↔ open palm → single note ↔ full chord
  • raise / lower → tremolo (a volume wobble)

both hands

  • move apart / together → more / less reverb

extras

  • scale & key menus change the mood
  • the side ladder shows the chord you're playing
  • record captures a clip
